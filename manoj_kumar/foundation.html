Perfect your complexion with our extensive range of foundations, available in all textures from serum to powder. The foundation allows you to achieve an even complexion and hide skin imperfections if there are any. It facilitates easy application and blending of beauty products such as bronzer or blush. Moreover, the foundation, as the basis of all make-up, protects the skin from external factors and premature aging thanks to the vitamins and antioxidants it contains. Achieve the most natural-looking finish with the foundation from bareMinerals or camouflage skin with Dermablend. Discover a lit-from-within glow with our long-lasting, buildable coverage options.
